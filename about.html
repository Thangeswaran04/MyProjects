About the Institution

Founded in 2008, Sri. R. P. Sarathy by Smt. Mahalakshmi Educational Trust, R P Sarathy Institute of Technology represents a rich tradition of excellence in technology-based education. A premier-league institution among the affiliates of Anna university, owes its vision to late Sri. an Entrepreneur, Educationalist and Philanthropist. R P Sarathy Institute of Technology in its 12 years of existence, is a reputed Institution in the State of Tamilnadu...

About the Department

The primary objective of the Department of COMPUTER SCIENCE & ENGINEERING at RPSIT is to prepare our students to apply their technological skills to comprehend and analyze complex problems to design and implement the feasible solutions. Graduates acquire the desire for lifelong learning and the ability to work in multi-disciplinary teams in meeting the global challenges. Graduates exhibit professional ethics, skills for management and responsibility towards societal needs. With rapidly evolving technology and the continuous need for innovation the department has always produced quality professionals.
